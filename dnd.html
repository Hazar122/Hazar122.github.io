Animated Pumpkin

A seemingly normal looking pumpkin or jack-o'-latern that is infused with magic. These small constructs have the ability to hover about 10 feet off the ground. Once an animated pumpkin targets a foe it lifts from the ground and soars towards it where upon getting in range will explode into a blazing explosion. Animated Pumpkins that havent targeted a creature and are destroyed still explode upon being destroyed making these ticking death traps.
